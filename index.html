The Team Behind

At Bento Games, we like to center our production process around the people working on the project. We share ideas, brainstorm together and focus on coming out with brand new ideas never explore in a video games. Our goal is to not only make an interesting game for our players, but to make a milestone in the video game industry. Bento Games is still trying to make its place since the last 5 years. Each year, we expand a little more in order to make bigger and bigger projects. One day we hope to be in the same bag as our neighbour Ubisoft.

We love to give our employes the opportunity to spend as much time as they want on a project. Some spend more with their family and others work hard on the project even the weekend. We are really flexible with the way you want to work. If you enjoy spending time with colleagues, don't worry we got a lot of activities each weak. If you're interested in working at Bento Games and we're looking for artist or pogrammers, feel free to stop by the contact section!
